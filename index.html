What is Mah Jongg?

Mah Jongg is a game played with 144 tiles that originated in China in the 19th century. In the 20th century an Americanized version became popular here. It is a game of skill, luck, and concentration. The American version of Mah Jongg requires a player to purchase a new score card each year. The goal of the game is to make matching sets of tiles according to several options on the score card.
